Frequently Asked Questions (FAQs)

Is PhishGuard free?

PhishGuard offers a **free tier** with basic URL checking and limited history storage. Premium tiers are available for advanced features like PhishMail integration and unlimited history tracking.

How accurate is the detection?

Our system uses a proprietary machine learning model trained on millions of data points, giving it a verified accuracy rate of over **98%** in detecting known and zero-day phishing threats.

Does PhishGuard store my personal data?

No. We strictly **do not store** personally identifiable information (PII). We only store the URLs you submit and the resulting verdict for model improvement and your personal history log.

Troubleshooting & Common Issues

I'm getting an 'Error: URL Not Found' message.

Ensure you have copied the **complete URL**, including the http:// or https:// protocol. The system cannot analyze partial addresses or domain names alone.

The detection process is running slowly.

Most checks take less than one second. If you experience slowdowns, please try clearing your browser cache. If the issue persists, contact support with your browser details.
